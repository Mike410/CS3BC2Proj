Who We Are And What We Do.


Kickoff clothing prides itself on being both stylish as well as ethical. All our clothing is locally sourced and produced in Kenya. Each garment we produce is full of character, and we can trace each individual piece of clothing back to the supplier of the fabric and the tailor who lovingly created your colourful garment. We believe that fair treatment and fair payment to our partners in Kenya will result in superior quality clothing as well as aiding the development of the local community.

Our current clothing line consists of the super-stylish and ultra-comfy kikoy pants and shorts. We decided to launch the Kikoff brand with these classic takes on the kikoy fabric due to their huge popularity in Kenya, especially on the coast in areas such as Mombasa and Malindi. With multiple colours and designs available, you can pick the one that really stands out to you!
